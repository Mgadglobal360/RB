Fixed Assets
Physical verification of fixed assets
A physical asset count is performed at least once every 2 years for Factories and once every 3 years for office locations.

Upon completion of the count, a reconciliation is performed to the fixed asset register (FAR) with differences investigated and resolved in a timely manner.

Updates required to the FAR must be approved by Finance Director prior to processing by Shared Services/local Finance Controller.

Final review and sign-off the physical count report/ reconciliation to be completed by the Finance Director.

Fixed Assets
Annual impairment review
A review is performed at least annually of major assets to ensure useful economic lives (and therefore depreciation and re-evaluation) and carrying values are appropriate and in compliance with the Finance Manual B15 and local requirements. All changes in Useful Economic Life outside of standard policy must be approved by Finance Director/ Supply Director or Regional Finance Director.

This should include a review for indicators of impairment to determine any write off of the difference between the recoverable amount and the recorded cost. Write-offs, impairments, brand life changes are subject to approval according to thresholds defined in Finance Manual B15.
