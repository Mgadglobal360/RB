R2R.02.07 / COR / R2R / Aug-2021 Submitted Back
Activity :Fixed Assets
Control :Monthly review of capitalised costs in projects
A periodic monthly review of projects included in the ERP system is performed (in detail) to ensure that invoices have been allocated correctly and only asset costs are allocated to the project (e.g. no internal labour or expense items or Repairs & Maintenance costs). Subsequent costs should only be capitalised if they improve the useful life of the asset and the cost of the item can be measured reliably (IAS 16).
Criteria :1. Determine that a process is in place to ensure projects are reviewed periodically and invoices are allocated to the projects correctly.

2. Review the fixed asset register and all capitalisations made. If costs for repairs were capitalised, verify that the repairs fulfil the criteria set out in Finance Manual policy B15.
